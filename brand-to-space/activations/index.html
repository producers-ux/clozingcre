The Elrod — Launch Day

850 N Palm Canyon Drive · Palm Springs, CA

This studio was Arthur Elrod's. Constructed between 1968 and 1970, it sits at the center of what would become Palm Springs' Uptown Design District — a neighborhood that, in many ways, Elrod helped define. His clients were Hollywood royalty. He dressed the homes of Walt Disney and Lucille Ball. Frank Sinatra. Bob Hope.

Elrod didn't just decorate rooms. He shaped a vision of desert living that still echoes through every wishlist, every mood board, every renovation in the Coachella Valley today. This building was where that work happened.

It's now available for lease. We're opening the doors — date TBD, pending building owner approval.

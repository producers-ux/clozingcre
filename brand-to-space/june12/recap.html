Event Recap

Where the right space
finds its people.

On the afternoon of June 12, 2026, Open + Range opened the doors of 850 N Palm Canyon Drive — the former studio of legendary designer Arthur Elrod — to an invited gathering of buyers, brand principals, designers, and cultural figures from across Palm Springs and the Coachella Valley.

Presented in partnership with the Palm Springs Cultural Center, the event was intentionally quiet. No signage. No pitches. Just champagne, the original riveted entry door, the architectural lighted ceiling that Elrod specified himself, and 4,180 square feet of mid-century commercial history that the city hasn’t seen activated in years.

“You put the right people in the right room and something happens that a brochure can’t do.”

— Kate Pawlicki Bourne, Clozing CRE Principal

Guests moved freely through the ground-floor showroom and into the adjacent offices — imagining the space as a flagship retail destination, a design studio, a gallery, a wellness concept. Several high-quality tenant conversations began that afternoon and are continuing. The space is still available.

If you attended and want to continue the conversation — or if you missed the event and would like a private tour — reach out directly to the Clozing CRE team.

The designer who
built this studio.

Arthur Elrod arrived in Palm Springs in 1947 and spent the next two decades defining what the desert interior could be. His firm shaped the interiors of the most celebrated mid-century homes in the Coachella Valley, working alongside architects including John Lautner, William Cody, Donald Wexler, and Hugh Kaptur.

850 N Palm Canyon Drive was his studio from 1968. The building’s architectural lighted ceiling, the original riveted entry door, the proportions of the showroom — all reflect the design language his firm brought to the corridor and to the world. This is one of the last remaining spaces directly connected to his practice. It is ready for its next chapter.

Open + Range

Brand activation for
remarkable spaces.

Open + Range is a program within Clozing CRE at Windermere Commercial Palm Springs. We take vacant or transitional commercial properties and create cultural activations that introduce them to the right audience before a traditional listing can.

June 12 was the first Open + Range activation of 850 N Palm Canyon. If you have a commercial space that deserves this kind of attention, let’s talk.

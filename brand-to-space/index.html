The Practice

What is Open + Range?

Open + Range is Clozing CRE's Brand-to-Space practice — a framework for matching the right brands, makers, and cultural operators to the right commercial spaces.

We don't just lease space. We think about who belongs in it, what story they tell, and why that matters to the property and the corridor it sits on.

Rooted in Palm Springs and the Coachella Valley. Operating since 2026.

Commercial Space Activation

We activate underutilized commercial properties with programming, pop-ups, and brand partnerships — creating proof-of-concept tenancies that de-risk leasing decisions and generate market heat.

Range Series No. 01 · Palm Springs, CA

Still Light at 850:
A Lie-Down Meditation Series Opens at the Historic Arthur Elrod Studio

Open + Range announces the inaugural activation of its Range programming series — a six-week meditation and sound experience held beneath the lighted ceiling of 850 N Palm Canyon Drive, in the presence of works by figurative artist Jenna Marie Hansen.

Palm Springs, CA — [Month] [Date], 2026

Open + Range, a commercial space activation program launched under Windermere Commercial Palm Springs, announces Still Light — the first installment of its Range series — opening at 850 N Palm Canyon Drive in June 2026. The four-month program transforms the historically significant Arthur Elrod studio into a lie-down meditation and sound environment, using the building's own architectural lighted ceiling as its central feature.

Sessions run daily in three formats: morning guided meditation, midday sound bath, and late-afternoon breathwork. All are conducted with participants lying on floor mats, looking up. Capacity is kept deliberately small — twelve to twenty participants — and sessions are ticketed or donation-based, with walk-up availability throughout the run.

The activation is designed around a specific architectural observation: 850 N Palm Canyon Drive has a lighted ceiling built into the structure of the building — a feature that has functioned as background in most previous tenancies. A lie-down session format makes the ceiling the primary visual and experiential element of every session. Participants recline and look directly up; the room becomes an interior sky.

"The ceiling was always the point. We are simply the first use of the space that treats it that way."

— Open + Range

Works by San Francisco-based figurative artist Jenna Marie Hansen are present throughout the activation. Hansen's practice centers on continuous line work — gesture as language, the body as carrier of emotion and connection. Her figures occupy the space as witnesses rather than objects, creating a relational environment for participants who may encounter her work before, during, or after a session.

The format draws from a demonstrable shift in how Los Angeles-based operators are activating commercial space. District Vision, the independent performance eyewear and apparel brand, opened its first-ever flagship in LA's Arts District in May 2026 as a "hybrid retail and research environment," building daily meditation, breathwork, and movement sessions into the floor plan. The format has been validated at street level; Still Light takes the same logic and removes the retail layer entirely — the room is the offering.

Still Light is the inaugural activation of Open + Range's Range series, a curated programming initiative that uses temporarily vacant or underutilized commercial spaces in the Coachella Valley for arts and wellness programming. The June–September window serves a specific strategic function: it keeps 850 activated and in public view while long-term tenant outreach runs in parallel. Print publications in the regional and national lifestyle space operate on lead times of two to four months; a four-month activation window is long enough to appear in fall editorial cycles that a shorter pop-up would miss entirely. The goal is to draw attention to the space — and give prospective tenants a functioning, attended room to walk into rather than an empty floor plan to imagine.

About

About Open + Range

Open + Range is a commercial space activation program incubated by Windermere Commercial Palm Springs. Through two distinct tracks — Open (commercial space activation) and Range (visual art and design programming) — O+R puts temporarily vacant and underutilized commercial properties to work for the benefit of the building, the corridor, and the community. The pilot program launches June 2026 at 850 N Palm Canyon Drive, Palm Springs. clozingcre.com/brand-to-space/

About Jenna Marie Hansen

Jenna Marie Hansen is a San Francisco-based figurative artist working in acrylic. Her practice centers on continuous line work that traces the energetics of the human body — gesture as language, figure as a site of emotional and spiritual meaning. She has exhibited independently and in group shows in the Bay Area and beyond. jennamariehansen.com · @jennamariehansen

About 850 N Palm Canyon Drive

850 N Palm Canyon Drive is a commercially significant address in downtown Palm Springs, California — the former studio of architect and designer Arthur Elrod. The building's architectural features, including its lighted ceiling, reflect the mid-century design sensibility for which Palm Springs is internationally recognized. The property is located on the primary commercial corridor of Palm Springs' downtown district.

About Windermere Commercial Palm Springs
